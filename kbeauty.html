    • Review. Primera Miracle Seed Essence

    The reason why I wanted so badly to start with this product is becasue I haven't seen many reviews of it, and I don't understand why! The first interesting thing, is the brand. Under the arm of the almighty Amore Pacific, Primera is an eco-oriented cosmetic brand.Therefore, they use mostly FSC-certified paper for its packaging and prints in natural soy ink. In addition, the product information and instruction manuals are printed on the paperboard box to reduce any unnecessary waste of resources. In general, Primera manufactures products that do not include paraben, synthetic pigments, artificial fragrances; primera uses a minimal amount of artificial chemical ingredients. That sounded like a win-win for sensible skins out there like mine.

    Now, the product. I was in a desperate search of something that will hydrate my skin without breaking it out, while treating my acne scars and my redness. At first I thought it was imposible to find that in one and only product. But then I came across this Liah Yoo video . So I decided to investigate a little. The first thing I was impresed with was the short list of ingredients:

    Nelumbo Nucifera Germ Extract (Lotus Seed Extract), Niacinamide(Vitamin B3), Glycereth-26, Glycerin, Ethylhexylglycerin, Disodium Edta, Adenosine, Sodium Hyaluronate, Saccharomyces Ferment, Methyl Gluceth-20, Citric Acid, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Citrate, 1,2-hexanediol.
